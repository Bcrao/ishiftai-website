Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an engagement typically take?

Engagement timelines vary based on project scope. Discovery and assessment typically takes 2-4 weeks. Implementation can range from 2-6 months or longer for complex transformations. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with companies of all sizes?

We specialize in enterprise-scale implementations and complex projects. We work with large enterprises, mid-market companies, and their partners. Our approach is tailored to your organizational size and needs.

What industries do you serve?

We have extensive experience across Financial Services, Healthcare, Retail, Manufacturing, Technology, Telecommunications, Energy, and Government sectors. Our expertise spans multiple industries.

Can you help with existing AI projects?

Absolutely. We provide assessments, optimization, and enhancement of existing AI implementations. If you're looking to improve or scale an existing solution, we can help.

Do you offer training and knowledge transfer?

Yes, knowledge transfer and team training are core parts of our engagement. We ensure your team is equipped to maintain and evolve the solutions we implement.

What about ongoing support?

We provide support options ranging from project-based consulting to ongoing advisory relationships. We can discuss support arrangements based on your needs.
